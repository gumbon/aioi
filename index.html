Overview

You can enable which indicators to display. They are made to support your analysis as much as possible with a simple beautiful interface without messing up the chart.

Breakout Prediction

The indicator will show its prediction of whether the price will break the previous high/low with the win rate.

Market Structure

Show the market structure (BOS & CHoCH levels)
To detect the next buy/sell stops levels or whether market structure shifted or not.

Time Separators

5 type of separatos: Anchor Time (a vertical line at a specific time), Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Quarterly.

Price Levels

NWOG (New Week Opening Gap), Previous 1H/4H/Day/Week/Month H/L/C H/L/C, ADR H/L, Price line & price zone at a specific time (O/C/H/L)

SMT Divergence

SMT (Smart Money Technique) Divergence. Display divergence between selected symbol like S&P500 (ES) vs NASDAQ100 (NQ).

Magic Bands

This help you alot in identifying the current trend, potential entry, profit taking and also stop loss points.

Accumulation Zone

The market tends to reprice the higher/lower to the old high/low or imbalance/fair value price.

Lookback Data Ranges

This can display high/low/mid of the daily or 4h data ranges

Magic Bars

The indicator has Fractal Bars, Imbalance Bars (Fair Value Gap) and Trend Bars.

Trendlines

A simple & widely used analysis approach for many traders.

Session & STD

6 sessions (Asian, London Open, New York Open, RDR, ODR, ADR) & Standard Deviations

Moving Average

One of the most used indicators for every trader. It can show up to 4 MAs & bands of 1 MA.

Moving Average Ribbon

Ribbon showed by 4 MA values.

Zig Zag

This can show up to 2 ZigZag lines.
This is suitable for traders who have difficulty in detecting key levels of the prices.

VWAP

VWAP with 2 options (Anchored or Rolling) & Stdev Bands.

Statistic Panel

There are 5 panels: Session Info, Previous D/W Breakout Info, Money Management, 2 Trend Panels
