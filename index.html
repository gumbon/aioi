Overview

You can enable which indicators to display. They are made to support your analysis as much as possible with a simple beautiful interface without messing up the chart.

Time Separators

5 type of separatos: Anchor Time (a vertical line at a specific time), Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Quarterly.

Price Levels

Previous Day or Previous Week H/L/C H/L/C, ADR H/L, Price line at a specific time (O/C/H/L)

Accumulation Zone

The market tends to reprice the higher/lower to the old high/low or imbalance/fair value price.

Lookback Data Ranges

This can display high/low/mid of the daily or 4h data ranges

Magic Bars

The indicator has Fractal Bars & Imbalance Bars (Fair Value Gap).

Trendlines

A simple & widely used analysis approach for many traders.

Session & STD

3 sessions (Asian, London Open, New York Open) & Standard Deviations

Moving Average

One of the most used indicators for every trader. It can show up to 4 MAs & bands of 1 MA.

Direction Box

A box with vertical & horizontal midlines for easily analysis price direction. It also can show lines connecting between lows or highs.

Zig Zag

This can show up to 2 ZigZag lines.
This is suitable for traders who have difficulty in detecting key levels of the prices.

Statistic Panel

There are 4 panels: Session Info, Money Management, 2 Trend Panels

Linear Regression Channel

The Linear Regression Channel is a three-line technical indicator which is mainly used to determine trend direction.

Old High/Low

Show the last n old high/low lines.
To detect the next buy/sell stops levels or whether market structure shifted or not.
